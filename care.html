  Saying hello

Thank you for looking after me! I might be shy when we first meet, and even more so if it's a new environment. Please give me time to get used to you!

When approaching a new cat, don't be too aggressive or enthusiastic - this can be scary! Instead, offer your hand out (fist closed) âœŠ and let us sniff it. Don't stare at us, either - doing a slow blink when we look at you lets us know that you're not a threat.

  Food and water

I am perfectly disciplined with kibble and water; just leave a bowl of each out and I will slowly nibble away at them for a day - or two or three.

I absolutely adore treats, canned wet food, cheese, and leafy greens. Reward me for good behavior! Buy new brands or flavors at your own risk; I'm picky and there's a paw-sibility I won't like your new offerings.

  Rest

I like to take naps in the late afternoon, late evening, and early morning. I'm happiest when humans return after a long day, and/or right after I've had a good long nap.

  Grooming

Are you kitten me? I groom myself pretty well and don't usually need (or want) baths. I love being brushed!

  Litter

I don't have digestion issues and I never have accidents. Un-fur-tunately for you, I'd ask that you please refresh my litter box every 3-4 days at mew-nimum.
